Chosenfewrecords is a Hamburg-based music label specializing in Hiphop, Dancehall, and RnB.

Our mission is to bring the best of Hiphop, Dancehall, and RnB to the world. We invite you to join us on this journey and experience the Chosenfewrecords sound.
